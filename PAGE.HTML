London

London is the capital of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitanats.

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millenia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.

DOUALA

Douala is the chief metropolis of Cameroon. It has a population of over five million people. The seaport and airport make Douala the main entry and exit town for people and goods.

As a regional and divisional headquarters, the Governor of the Littoral and the SDO of Wouri reside here.

Yaounde

Yaounde is the capital of Cameroon. It has a population of about 3-5 million people

Being a major city, the President of Cameroon and most of the ministers reside in that city
